Weather App

About project

Experience real-time weather updates with our MERN stack Weather App. This dynamic application provides users with accurate weather forecasts powered by the MongoDB, Express.js, React, and Node.js (MERN) stack. Seamlessly integrating modern technologies, users can effortlessly check weather conditions, forecasts, and trends for their desired locations. With an intuitive user interface and responsive design, our Weather App ensures accessibility across various devices, offering a seamless weather-tracking experience.
